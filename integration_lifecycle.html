Integration Lifecycle

Ready to collaborate? Check out how we engage with you.

Things to consider (but not limited to) when developing an integrations component or service:

  • What data are you sharing?
  • Who are your users?
  • What kind of data security do you need?
  • Are you looking at connecting one application to another? Or are multiple applications involved?
  • What are your existing processes?
  • Why do you need an integration service?
  • Do you have funding?
  • Who will be part of the devops team?
  • Have you thought about what kind of support you need?
  • Do you have any constraints?
