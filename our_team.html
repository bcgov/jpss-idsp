Integration Delivery Service Deployments

We like to deploy often and in small iterative chunks. This way we can ensure all of our code is checked as thoroughly as possible and any gotcha’s are found sooner.

Collaboratively! We work with business area SME’s, vendor teams and ISB SME’s to get changes made quickly, efficiently and as smooth as possible.

Current State

While we wait for the Integration Delivery Service team to be fully staffed, we are working with the business area vendor teams and ISB resources to get deployments completed through DEV, TEST and PROD.

Future State

When the Integration Delivery Service team is fully staffed (funding dependent), we will be able to complete most if not all devops activities ourselves while working in partnership with business areas and/or vendor teams.
